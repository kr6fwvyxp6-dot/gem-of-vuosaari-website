House rules, terms & cancellation

Everything worth knowing before you stay.

House rules

  • Check-in: from 15:00 · Check-out: by 11:00
  • Maximum occupancy: 4 guests
  • Smoking: not permitted indoors; permitted only on the patio at the apartment entrance
  • Pets: not allowed
  • Parties or events: not permitted
  • Quiet hours: 22:00–08:00, out of respect for neighbours

Terms & conditions

Booking & payment

Full payment is due before the start of your stay. We accept bank transfer, credit card, and invoice. If you book via Airbnb or Booking.com, payment is handled by that platform according to its own terms.

Damage & liability

You're responsible for any damage to the apartment, furniture, or equipment beyond normal wear and tear during your stay. No separate security deposit is charged when you book. If damage occurs, we'll be in touch after checkout with photos and the repair or replacement cost, and may charge this to the payment method used for your booking — or, for Airbnb and Booking.com stays, through that platform's damage resolution process. Please let us know about breakages or damage as soon as they happen.

Cancellation policy

Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. Cancellations made less than 7 days before check-in, and no-shows, are non-refundable. If you booked via Airbnb or Booking.com, that platform's cancellation policy applies instead, since each platform enforces its own rules for bookings made there.
